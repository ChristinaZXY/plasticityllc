Why "Plasticity"

Named for the way the mind grows.

Plasticity is the brain's capacity to reshape itself in response to experience — the biological root of how we learn and improve.

As AI takes on more of the thinking that used to be ours, the human capacities that matter most are the ones that keep us adaptable: attention, learning, innovation. We build tools that make people more capable, not more dependent on the machine.

Our journey begins with LittleWing, at the very start of life, where plasticity is greatest and the habits of a lifetime take root. Over time, we're building a family of products that strengthens human ability across the whole arc of a life.
