Why "Plasticity"

Named for the way growing minds are made.

Plasticity is the brain's capacity to reshape itself in response to experience — and it is never greater than in the first years of life. That window is the reason we exist.

We build for the parents and caregivers holding that window open: software that informs without overwhelming, remembers without prying, and stays out of the way of the moments that matter.

We're small on purpose. It lets us care about the details that make a tool feel calm rather than clever.

"Make the daily work of raising a child feel a little lighter, a little calmer, and a lot less lonely."

— The idea behind everything we ship.
